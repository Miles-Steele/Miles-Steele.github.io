Jellyfish Fields Forever is a new AR phone app project from Nickelodeon intended to compete with similar branded AR apps such as Pokemon Go and Monster Hunter Now. The app will allow users to work together in the real world to catch roaming jellyfish, which can be seen through their phone cameras. The game has a focus on cooperative play, community building, and gameafying physical health.
